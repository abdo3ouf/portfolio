About

I'm a Mechatronics Engineering student at Nile University, Giza — on a full academic scholarship — with a deep passion for multidisciplinary engineering systems.

My work spans the full stack of engineering: designing and routing PCBs, writing embedded firmware in C++, implementing feedback control algorithms, and modeling mechanical assemblies in CAD.

Whether it's a closed-loop motor controller or an autonomous robot, I enjoy projects where precision matters and every layer of the system has to talk to every other.
